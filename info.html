Safety Measures and Protocols for Customers

Because safety is the key for a remarkable experience!

Your safety is our priority. Here are some of the measures taken to keep everyone safe while having fun:

  • Mandatory temperature checks and facemasks are required upon entry.
  • Hand sanitizers and foot baths are to be used.
  • Social distancing markers are implemeted.
  • Frequent cleaning and sanitation of surfaces is to done.
  • Stricter food safety standards and practices are to be observed.

Restaurants has the right to deny entry and services to those who refuse to follow social distancing and safety protocols. #Besafe
